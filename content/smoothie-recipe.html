The Ultimate Heart-Healthy Smoothie

A budget-friendly breakfast designed to tackle high cholesterol. Nutrient-dense, high in fiber, and genuinely delicious.

The Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 scoop Protein Powder (Unflavored)
  • 1 tbsp Almond Butter (Unsweetened)
  • 2 tbsp Greek Yogurt (Plain, Non-fat)
  • 1 cup Frozen Mixed Berries
  • 2-3 cups Fresh Spinach
  • 1 cup Water or Unsweetened Almond Milk

Instructions

  1. Combine all ingredients in a blender. For best results, add liquids first.
  2. Blend on high for 60-90 seconds until completely smooth. Add more water if needed to reach desired consistency.
  3. Enjoy immediately!

Why This Works

Each ingredient is chosen for its specific heart-health benefits.

  • ✔
    Almond Butter: Provides healthy monounsaturated fats, crucial for lowering LDL ("bad") cholesterol.
  • ✔
    Berries & Spinach: Packed with soluble fiber and antioxidants. Soluble fiber is essential for binding to cholesterol and removing it from your body.
  • ✔
    Greek Yogurt: Adds a boost of protein for satiety and contains probiotics for gut health, which plays a role in overall metabolic wellness.
