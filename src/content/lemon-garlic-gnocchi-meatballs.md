            <p class="text-xl text-slate-600 font-light mb-8">
                Gnocchi is usually a "treat" meal‚Äîheavy on butter, cheese, and refined carbs. But with a few strategic swaps, we can turn this comfort food into a lipid-lowering powerhouse.
            </p>
            <h3 class="font-serif text-2xl text-slate-900 mt-10 mb-4">The "Fiber Sponge" Technique</h3>
            <p>
                The problem with most sheet pan meals is that they lack <strong>soluble fiber</strong>. By adding a can of Cannellini beans directly to the roasting pan, we introduce a powerful binding agent.
            </p>
            <p>
                These beans get crispy on the outside and creamy on the inside, soaking up the lemon-garlic juices. More importantly, they bind to bile acids in your gut, forcing your liver to pull LDL cholesterol out of your bloodstream to replace them.
            </p>
            <p>
                We also swapped the traditional butter sauce for <strong>Extra Virgin Olive Oil</strong> to drastically cut saturated fat while keeping that rich mouthfeel.
            </p>
        </div>
