The 14-Hour Reset

By finishing dinner at 6:00 PM and holding until 8:00 AM, we create a natural 14-hour fasting window. This allows the digestive system to rest fully before sleep, which can improve heart rate variability (HRV) and insulin sensitivity the next morning.
