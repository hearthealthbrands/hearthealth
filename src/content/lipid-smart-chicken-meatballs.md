                    <div>
                        <h4 class="font-bold text-slate-900 text-lg">The "Lean" Swap (Liver Defense)</h4>
                        <p class="text-slate-600 leading-relaxed mt-1">
                            We use 99% lean chicken breast instead of dark meat. Saturated fat "downregulates" (turns off) the LDL receptors in your liver. By keeping the fat low, we keep those receptors active, allowing your body to clear cholesterol naturally.
                        </p>
                    </div>
                </div>

                <div class="flex gap-5 items-start">
                    <span class="flex-shrink-0 w-10 h-10 bg-white rounded-full flex items-center justify-center text-xl shadow-sm">üßΩ</span>
                    <div>
                        <h4 class="font-bold text-slate-900 text-lg">The "Sponge" Effect</h4>
                        <p class="text-slate-600 leading-relaxed mt-1">
                            By pairing this with chickpea or whole wheat pasta, we introduce soluble fiber. In the gut, this fiber acts like a sponge, binding to bile acids (made of cholesterol) and removing them before they can enter your bloodstream.
                        </p>
                    </div>
                </div>

                <div class="flex gap-5 items-start">
                    <span class="flex-shrink-0 w-10 h-10 bg-white rounded-full flex items-center justify-center text-xl shadow-sm">ü•ú</span>
                    <div>
                        <h4 class="font-bold text-slate-900 text-lg">The Almond Shield</h4>
                        <p class="text-slate-600 leading-relaxed mt-1">
                            We replaced white breadcrumbs with almond flour. Almonds contain plant sterols, which structurally resemble cholesterol. They compete for absorption in the gut, effectively blocking real cholesterol from getting in.
                        </p>
                    </div>
                </div>
