Breakfast Recipe

The Ultimate Heart-Healthy Smoothie

A delicious breakfast designed to tackle high cholesterol. Nutrient-dense, high in soluble fiber, and genuinely delicious.

Calories

~313

Protein

35g

Fiber

9g

Ingredients

  • 1 scoop Protein Powder (Unflavored)
  • 1 tbsp Almond Butter (Unsweetened)
  • 2 tbsp Greek Yogurt (Plain, Non-fat)
  • 1 cup Frozen Mixed Berries
  • 2-3 cups Fresh Spinach
  • 1 cup Water or Almond Milk

Instructions

  1. 1 Combine all ingredients in a blender. For best results, add liquids first.
  2. 2 Blend on high for 60-90 seconds until completely smooth.
  3. 3 Pour into a glass and enjoy immediately!

Prep Tip

You can prep the dry ingredients and the spinach in zip-lock bags and freeze them for quick morning access.

Why This Works for Heart Health

Each ingredient is chosen for its specific cholesterol-lowering and heart-protective benefits.

  • Almond Butter A rich source of healthy monounsaturated fats, which are crucial for lowering LDL ("bad") cholesterol.
  • Berries & Spinach Packed with soluble fiber and antioxidants. Soluble fiber binds to cholesterol in the digestive system and removes it from your body.
  • Greek Yogurt Adds a boost of protein for satiety (feeling full) and contains probiotics for gut health.
