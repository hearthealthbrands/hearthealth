Beginner Plan

Your First 30 Days: A Simple Guide

This isn't a list of strict rules. It is a clear, week-by-week timeline to help you take control with confidence.

Week 1

Focus on Addition, Not Subtraction

Start by adding powerful, LDL-lowering foods. Soluble fiber acts like a sponge, binding to cholesterol and removing it.

  • Start your day with a bowl of oatmeal or barley.
  • Swap one snack for an apple, pear, or edamame.
  • Add a side of Brussels sprouts to one dinner.
Week 2

Master the Fats

Fat isn't the enemyâ€”the type of fat is what matters. Swap saturated "bad" fats for heart-healthy monounsaturated ones.

  • Switch from butter to olive oil for cooking.
  • Add a quarter avocado to salads or toast.
  • Snack on a small handful of almonds or walnuts.
Week 3

Power Up with Plants

You don't need to be vegetarian, but swapping some meat for plants is highly effective.

  • Try "Meatless Monday" (lentils or tofu instead of beef).
  • Look for foods fortified with plant sterols.
Week 4

Build Your System

Create a sustainable routine that eliminates daily stress and decision-making.

  • Identify 1 "Go-To" breakfast you can make in 5 minutes.
  • Master 3 simple, heart-healthy dinners.
