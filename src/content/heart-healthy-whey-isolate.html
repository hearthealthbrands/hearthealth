The Ion Exchange Compromise

Some popular "naked" brands utilize Ion Exchange. While this produces high protein yields, the chemical reagents deplete Lactoferrin, Alpha-Lactalbumin, and Glycomacropeptides (GMP). These native fractions are essential for vascular tone and immune modulation.[6]

The Sodium Trap

Not all unflavored isolates are heart-safe. Brands like Opportuniteas show levels as high as 170mg per serving. For an athlete consuming 2-3 scoops daily, this adds over 500mg of "invisible" sodium, counter-productive to blood pressure management.
