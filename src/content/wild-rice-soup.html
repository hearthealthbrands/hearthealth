Cozy Autumn Wild Rice Soup

Before the holiday indulgence begins, we "lock in" our health with this nutrient-dense bowl. Loaded with kale, mushrooms, and heart-healthy wild rice.

Recipe Adapted from Gimme Some Oven

Plant Power

100% Vegan

Key Grain

Wild Rice

Focus

Immunity

"Thanksgiving is a marathon. This soup is the training."

With the holidays approaching, the goal isn't to deprive yourself on Thursday. The goal is to enter the week with a foundation of nutrient density.

We chose this recipe from Gimme Some Oven because it relies on Wild Rice (technically a grass, higher in protein and fiber than brown rice) and a massive amount of Kale and Mushrooms (essential for immune support during travel season).

The Shopping List

  • 6 cups Vegetable Stock
  • 1 cup Wild Rice (Uncooked)
  • 8 oz Baby Bella Mushrooms (Sliced)
  • 1 can (14oz) Coconut Milk (The secret to creaminess)
  • 2 cups Kale (Chopped, stems removed)
  • 1 Sweet Potato (Peeled/Diced)
  • 1 Onion, 2 Carrots, 2 Celery Ribs
  • Old Bay Seasoning (1.5 tbsp) & Garlic (4 cloves)

The Protocol (Stovetop)

  1. 1 Sauté: In a large pot, sauté onion in olive oil (5 mins). Add garlic for 1 min.
  2. 2 Simmer: Add stock, wild rice, mushrooms, carrots, celery, sweet potato, and Old Bay. Bring to boil, then cover and simmer on low for 40 mins.
  3. 3 Finish: Stir in the coconut milk and kale. Cook 5 more mins until kale is tender.
  4. 4 Serve: Season generously with salt & pepper.
