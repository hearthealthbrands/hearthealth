                                The Shopping List
                            </h3>
                            <ul class="space-y-3 text-slate-700 text-sm">
                                <li class="pb-2 border-b border-slate-50"><strong>6 cups</strong> TJ's Organic Free Range Chicken Broth</li>
                                <li class="pb-2 border-b border-slate-50"><strong>1 cup</strong> Wild Rice (Uncooked)</li>
                                <li class="pb-2 border-b border-slate-50"><strong>8 oz</strong> Phillips Gourmet Baby Bella Mushrooms (Sliced)</li>
                                <li class="pb-2 border-b border-slate-50"><strong>1 can (14oz)</strong> TJ's Organic Reduced Fat Coconut Milk</li>
                                <li class="pb-2 border-b border-slate-50"><strong>2 cups</strong> TJ's Organic Shredded Kale</li>
                                <li class="pb-2 border-b border-slate-50"><strong>1</strong> Sweet Potato (Peeled/Diced)</li>
                                <li class="pb-2 border-b border-slate-50"><strong>1</strong> Onion, <strong>2</strong> TJ's Organic Whole Carrots, <strong>2</strong> Celery Ribs</li>
                                <li><strong>Old Bay Seasoning</strong> (1.5 tbsp) & Garlic (4 cloves)</li>
                            </ul>
                        </div>

                        <div>
                            <h3 class="text-xl font-serif font-bold text-slate-900 mb-6 flex items-center gap-2">
                                <svg class="w-5 h-5 text-primary" fill="none" stroke="currentColor" viewBox="0 0 24 24"><path stroke-linecap="round" stroke-linejoin="round" stroke-width="2" d="M12 6v6h45m-4-2a2 2 0 100-4m0 4a2 2 0 110-4m0 4v2m0-6V4"></path></svg>
                                The Protocol (Stovetop)
                            </h3>
                            <ol class="space-y-6 text-slate-700 list-none relative text-sm">
                                <li class="relative pl-8">
                                    <span class="absolute left-0 top-0 flex items-center justify-center w-6 h-6 rounded-full bg-slate-100 text-xs font-bold text-slate-600">1</span>
                                    <strong>Sauté:</strong> In a large pot, sauté onion in olive oil (5 mins). Add garlic for 1 min.
                                </li>
                                <li class="relative pl-8">
                                    <span class="absolute left-0 top-0 flex items-center justify-center w-6 h-6 rounded-full bg-slate-100 text-xs font-bold text-slate-600">2</span>
                                    <strong>Simmer:</strong> Add chicken broth, wild rice, mushrooms, carrots, celery, sweet potato, and Old Bay. Bring to boil, then cover and simmer on low for 40 mins.
                                </li>
                                <li class="relative pl-8">
                                    <span class="absolute left-0 top-0 flex items-center justify-center w-6 h-6 rounded-full bg-slate-100 text-xs font-bold text-slate-600">3</span>
                                    <strong>Finish:</strong> Stir in the coconut milk and kale. Cook 5 more mins until kale is tender.
                                </li>
                                <li class="relative pl-8">
                                    <span class="absolute left-0 top-0 flex items-center justify-center w-6 h-6 rounded-full bg-slate-100 text-xs font-bold text-slate-600">4</span>
                                    <strong>Serve:</strong> Season generously with salt & pepper.
                                </li>
